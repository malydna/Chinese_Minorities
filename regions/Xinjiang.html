ئۈرۈمچى شەھىرى，Urumqi

It is the capital of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China, located in central Xinjiang. The city borders Changji Prefecture on the east and west sides, Turpan City to the southeast, Bayingoleng Prefecture to the southwest, Wujiaqu City to the northwest, and Altay Region to the north. Located at the northern foot of the middle section of the Tianshan Mountains, on the southern edge of the Junggar Basin, surrounded by mountains in the east, south, and west, the terrain is high in the south and low in the north, with Bogda Mountain in the east, Tiangeer Mountain in the south, Karazha Mountain in the west, and North It is the Gurbantungut Desert.

Urumqi is a national civilized city, a national garden city, a national dual-support model city, an excellent tourist city in China, and a national model city for national unity and progress. In 1954, Dihua was renamed Urumqi, which means "beautiful pasture" in Mongolian.

The city has a total area of ​​10,989 square kilometers and a population of 4.054 million
